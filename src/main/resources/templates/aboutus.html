An online examination system is the complete agenda of an online test that has multiple features and functionalities. The online examination system uses online exam software through which the tests are created, conducted, and evaluated. This type of examination system has multiple benefits few of them are that it eliminates the dependency on paper for the question and answer sheets, and eliminates any sort of manual workload which is too much in the case of an offline test. 20,000 Students Given Test Through This Website. An online test portal is a web-based platform designed to facilitate the administration and management of online examinations. It enables educators and administrators to create, conduct, and evaluate online tests and assessments with ease. The portal supports various question types, ensures secure and reliable infrastructure, and provides accessibility from anywhere. It benefits students and educators by offering convenience, flexibility, and personalized learning experiences. The portal is ideal for educational institutions, corporate training, certification exams, and research. It streamlines the assessment process, making it efficient, effective, and accessible. The portal also reduces administrative burdens, enhances assessment accuracy, and offers scalability and cost-effectiveness.
